# Z8V Matrix Community Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to building a respectful, professional, and innovation-focused community.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Respect all community members
- Encourage constructive discussions
- Support decentralized innovation
- Avoid harassment or abusive behavior
- Help create a positive environment

---

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior may result in warnings or removal from community spaces.

---

## Community Vision

Z8V Matrix supports open collaboration, intelligent infrastructure, and future AI-powered decentralized technologies.
